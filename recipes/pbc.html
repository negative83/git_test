Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

"A soft chewy cookie that is not too sweet. Seconds to make!"

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1⁄2 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients together.
  2. Drop by spoonful onto baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes at 325°F or 8-10 minutes at 350°.F.
